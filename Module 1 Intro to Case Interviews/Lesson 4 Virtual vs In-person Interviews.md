# Virtual vs In-person Interviews

## Virtual Interview

Items to have:
- Paper & pen
- Good audio visual environment, a light that is on you
- Headphones
- Generally sit in an environment where they can see what's behind
- No calculators

Tips:
- Clear verbal communication, because you're not showing what you're writing down
- "I have these following things in my structure, here's a few things from 1, 2, 3 etc"
- Sit still in your chair
- Pay attention to looking directly into the camera

## In-person Interview

- Small talk
- Talk through your structures, ready for the breaking of the veil
- Ready for some **PSP questions** - Positive, Specific, Personal
- Have questions ready
