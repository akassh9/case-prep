# What is a Case?

A case is a **problem solving method** that reflects how real business problems are solved.

## What Skills Does a Case Test?

1. Digital Assessment
2. Case Competitions
3. Case Interview

A case is a test of your **real world applied problem solving ability**.

How does a case test that? Real world skills with real world enthusiasm.

## 4 Core Skills Tested

1. **Understand the problem** - Scoping the problem. What is in scope and what isn't. Don't complicate things. Simplify, narrow down and clarify what needs to be done.
2. **Ability to structure a plan** - Actionable plan, take the simplified version and turn it into action.
3. **Analyze the data** - Figure out what data you need and how to use it. What do I want, what should it say.
4. **Recommending Findings** - Best advisors are the ones that don't just quote the numbers.
